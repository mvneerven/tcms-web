Background

At first sight, a successful software product simply stems from a combination of a perfect product-market fit, intuitive, fluid User Experience Design (UX), and of course, good marketing.

Below the surface however, team quality and efficiency, a streamlined development process, solid architectural design, good use of (Cloud) infrastructure, and constant attention to security and compliance are equally important.

The ISV Canvas Survey presented here assesses this Tech State of Startups, Scaleups and ISVs

ISV Canvas Areas

The survey is divided into 7 areas, and returns a weighted score on each of them.

  • Team & Process
  • Architecture & Data
  • Security, Privacy & Compliance
  • Non-Functional Requirements
  • Cost Management
  • Strategy & Governance
  • Go-to-Market

How it works

The ISV Canvas Maturity Survey provides a way to objectively assess the maturity of Startups, Scaleups and ISVs.

Obviously, a Startup in the MVP stage is quite different from an established ISV. Each stage requires focus on different areas, some aspects are essential in one but optional in the other, and each stage has its own risks and challenges.

The ISV Canvas Maturity Survey uses a carefully crafted algorithm to take all these aspects in consideration, by attributing more weight to certain aspects of software development in each phase. This is done per question asked, but also per area.

SWOT Analysis

Besides the scores the ISV Canvas survey calculates, it presents a SWOT Analysis for each of the areas, with guidance and further reading advice.
