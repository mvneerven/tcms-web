ISV Canvas Survey (BETA)

Welcome to ISV Canvas, an assessment tool based on the work of a Chief Technology Officer with 30 years of experience in software development, who has worked for and with numerous companies, ranging from early startups to Enterprise Software vendors, who has worked in Software Developer/Architect and in governance roles, and is now a Native Cloud and Semantic Data advocate.

Background

At first sight, a truly great software product stems from commercial vision, has a perfect product-market fit and focuses highly on a fluid and efficient User Experience Design (UX).

Behind the screens however, team quality and efficiency, a streamlined development process, solid architectural design and good use of (Cloud) infrastructure is equally important.

The ISV Canvas Survey presented here assesses the tech state of Startups, Scaleups and ISVs in a number of areas, and returns a weighted score on each of them:

  • Team & Process
  • Architecture & Data
  • Security, Privacy & Compliance
  • Non-Functional Requirements
  • Cost Management
  • Strategy & Governance
  • Go-to-Market

How it works

The ISV Canvas Maturity Survey provides a way to objectively assess the maturity of Startups, Scaleups and ISVs.

Obviously, a Startup in the MVP stage is quite different from an established ISV. Each stage requires focus on different areas, some aspects are essential in one but optional in the other, and each stage has its own risks and challenges.

The ISV Canvas Maturity Survey uses a carefully crafted algorithm to take all these aspects in consideration.
