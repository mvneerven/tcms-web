Background

What makes a successful software product? 

First, we need a perfect product-market fit, some intuitive, fluid User Experience Design (UX), and of course, good marketing.

Below the surface however, solid architectural design, good use of (Cloud) infrastructure for speed and scalability, team quality and efficiency, a streamlined development process, and constant attention to security and compliance are equally important.

The ISV Canvas Survey presented here assesses this Tech State of Startups, Scaleups and ISVs and checks for all these aspects that make up mature software development.

ISV Canvas Areas

The survey is divided into 7 areas, and returns a weighted score on each of them.

  • Team & Process
  • Architecture & Data
  • Security, Privacy & Compliance
  • Non-Functional Requirements
  • Cost Management
  • Strategy & Governance
  • Go-to-Market

How it works

The ISV Canvas Maturity Survey provides a way to objectively assess the maturity of Startups, Scaleups and ISVs.

Obviously, a Startup in the MVP stage is quite different from an established ISV. Each stage requires focus on different areas, some aspects are essential in one but optional in the other, and each stage has its own risks and challenges.

The ISV Canvas Maturity Survey uses a carefully crafted algorithm to take all these aspects in consideration, by attributing more weight to certain aspects of software development in each phase. This is done per question asked, but also per area.

SWOT Analysis

Besides the scores the ISV Canvas survey calculates, it presents a SWOT Analysis for each of the areas, based on software development best practices and the collective knowledge of a number of experts involved in ISV Canvas.

Guidance

An ISV Canvas content Wiki is maintained for providing more background and pointers to further reading on each of the facts deducted in the SWOT Analysis.
