About ISV Canvas

ISV Canvas is an initiative of Neerventure's Chief Technology Officer Marc van Neerven.

During the last 30 years, Marc has worked as Lead Developer, Architect and CTO for and with numerous companies, ranging from early startups to Enterprise Software vendors.

Marc currently helps Startups, Scaleups and ISVs in strategic advice and governance roles, and is a Native Cloud and Semantic Data advocate.

History

ISV Canvas started when Microsoft asked Neerventure, in 2017, to assist with assessing Startups that wanted to get BizSpark funding.

At the time, it was hard for Microsoft to assess the technical potential of Startups that requested access to the program, and there was no ROI (return on investment) model, so the 'Tech Canvas', as it was called back then, provided a quick scan of the technical abilities and maturity of the Startups.

The BizSpark program has been replaced by the Microsoft for Startups program in 2018.

The idea of a repeatable, objective way to do a high level asessment of Startups is still very valid however, and it's been getting more and more traction.
Also, it doesn't stop at the Startup level: assessing Scaleups & established ISVs in terms of technical maturity is equally valuable, for instance from the standpoint of Startup ecosystems, ISV programs, M&A and investors.

Opportunities

Actively involved in the development of the ISV Canvas are Microsoft (ISV program), a few Investors and companies in the M&A and Due Diligence field.
