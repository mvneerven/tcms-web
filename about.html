About ISV Canvas

ISV Canvas is an initiative of Chief Technology Officer Marc van Neerven.

The idea of a repeatable, objective way to do a high level asessment of Startups, Scaleups & ISVs has is getting more and more traction.

Actively involved in the development of the ISV Canvas are Microsoft (ISV program), a few Investors and companies in the M&A and Due Diligence field.

Technology

The ISV Canvas survey runs off static HTML served directly from Cloud Storage, with JavaScript and a Serverless Functions REST API.

It uses Azure AD B2C IDaaS (Identity as a Service) as its identity service.
